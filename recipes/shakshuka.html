HOW TO MAKE SHAKSHUKA

Description

Shakshuka is a North African/ Middle Eastern originated food . I grew up eating this every Saturday in my house hold so it holds many memories for me.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 4-6 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4-6 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, deep skillet or frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they become soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and diced bell pepper, and cook for a few more minutes until the pepper begins to soften.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes to the skillet, along with the ground cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Allow the mixture to simmer on low heat for about 10-15 minutes, or until the tomatoes break down and the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Make small wells in the tomato sauce and crack the eggs into each well. Cover the skillet with a lid and cook on low heat for about 5-7 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your desired level of doneness.
