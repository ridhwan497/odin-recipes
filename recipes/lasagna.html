HOW TO MAKE LASAGNA BOLOGNESE

Description

This is a lasagna bolognese recipe made with ground beef. It is 100% halal

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound halal ground beef
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for some heat)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cups shredded halal mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated halal Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.
  4. Add the halal ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces with a wooden spoon.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Add the water and mix well. Simmer the sauce for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of the greased baking dish. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread another layer of the meat sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of shredded halal mozzarella cheese and grated halal Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers, starting with the noodles and ending with a layer of cheese. You should be able to make 3 layers.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.
